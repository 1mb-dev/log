This site is one builder's log.

Markdown in git. A single Go binary called markgo. Caddy in front. Nothing else.

No platform. No follower count. No algorithm deciding who reads what. Posts land when you `git commit && make deploy`.

Your VPS. Your domain. Your stack. Maintenance is yours too.

If that shape fits how you want to write, run your own.

The path is in the [deployment guide](https://github.com/1mb-dev/log/blob/main/docs/deployment.md).
