Your stack runs on people you've never met.

Half the dependencies are maintained by one person.
Some by two.
A few haven't been updated in years, still running fine.

Every production system runs on libraries nobody was paid to write.

The database driver.
The JSON parser.
The http client.
The retry logic.
The date library.

Someone's weekend. Someone's itch. Someone's late-night commit that stuck around.

Nobody coordinated this.
No CEO. No roadmap. No quarterly goals.
Thousands of people, each shipping what they needed.

The whole thing held together by people building exactly like you.

Not nameless.
Just not organized.

Solo builds all the way down.
